How this works

Four ideas behind everything here.

This is not school at a desk. It is a way of living the day so that learning is woven into meals, play, walks, and bedtime.

01

The child leads

Follow what Zayn is drawn to. His attention is the curriculum. We set the table; he chooses the work.

02

The world is the classroom

A puddle, a spice jar, a grandparent's voice. Real objects and real people teach more than any screen.

03

Repeat, then stretch

Toddlers master through repetition. We repeat what he loves, then add one small new challenge.

04

Roots and wings together

Arabic, faith, and family heritage grow right alongside science and creativity, never as an afterthought.

The framework

The four pillars.

Every activity, book, and milestone in this curriculum belongs to one of these. Together they raise a whole child.

📖
Core academics

Language & Mind

Words, letters, numbers, and the thinking that comes before reading and math. Built from conversation up.

  • Talk all day: narrate, name, ask, wait for an answer
  • Read aloud daily, point to words and pictures
  • Counting, sorting, patterns, cause and effect
  • Memory, focus, and problem-solving games
🔭
Science & curiosity

Wonder & World

The scientist's instinct: notice, wonder, test, repeat. Nature, the senses, and how things work.

  • Daily time outdoors, in all weather
  • Sensory play: water, sand, textures, scents
  • Simple experiments and "what happens if?"
  • Animals, plants, weather, the body, the sky
🕌
Arabic & heritage

Roots & Faith

Arabic from the first word, the Qur'an as a familiar sound, and the values that shape a good character.

  • Arabic spoken and sung at home, daily
  • The 28 letters as a game, not a drill
  • Short surahs and du'as as part of the routine
  • Akhlaq: kindness, gratitude, honesty, respect
🎨
Creativity & character

Heart & Hands

Art, music, movement, and the emotional skills that matter more than any fact: patience, courage, empathy.

  • Open-ended art: crayons, paint, dough, glue
  • Music and rhythm every day
  • Gross and fine motor: climb, pour, pinch, build
  • Naming feelings and learning to wait
